## Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, symbols)
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps 
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages 
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone 
- Keep your apps and devices updated to prevent security vulnerabilities 
- Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store 
- Log out from shared/public computers and avoid saving passwords on them 
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi—avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts 

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
-	Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only required to send money 
-	Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve “collect requests” from strangers 
-	Always verify the identity of the person before making any payment 
-	Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for suspicious activity 

## Reporting & Help
-	Report cyber fraud immediately at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in 
-	Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for quick action on financial fraud)
