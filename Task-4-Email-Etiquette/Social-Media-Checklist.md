### Do's

- Think before posting---consider how it reflects on you long-term

- Maintain privacy settings and protect personal information

- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages

- Use social media to build your professional presence (LinkedIn, portfolios, etc.)

### Don'ts

- Don't share sensitive personal data (address, phone number, passwords)

- Don't engage in online arguments, trolling, or hate speech

- Don't post inappropriate or offensive content

- Don't believe or forward unverified news or rumors

- Don't overshare details that could harm your reputation or safety
